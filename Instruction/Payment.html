Your payment for this experiment consists of two parts. You will be paid automatically once you completed the experiment. The final amount depends on your decisions, the decisions of other participants, and, to some extent, chance. On average, the payment will be €12.

Throughout the experiment, you can earn or lose points. These points determine your final payment and are converted at a rate of 50 points = €1. The specific rules for earning points will be provided in the instructions before each part.

At the end of the experiment, you will learn how much you earned in each part and overall. Other participants will not learn how much you earned in this experiment.
